Fruit collector

How to play

You will be shown a random number at the start of the game.

There are four types of fruit. You will click on the fruit to get a random number.

Each fruit has a different value that will be shown when you click it.

The object of the game is to click on the fruit to try to match the random number shown at the begennig of the game.

If you match the random number you win!. If you go over the number you lose.

Each time you win or lose the game should restart with a different number.
